## Overview

The very first Cursor community meetup in Türkiye, held at CODE in Ankara. This event marked the official launch of the CursorTürkiye community, bringing together developers, AI enthusiasts, and tech professionals to explore the power of AI-assisted coding.

## Format

- **Type**: In-person meetup
- **Venue**: CODE, Ankara
- **Duration**: Evening session with networking
- **Speakers**: 3 (including a Cursor team member)
- **Structure**: Presentations + live demos + Q&A + networking

## Highlights

- Dan Perks from the Cursor team joined remotely to share insights directly from the source
- Live demonstrations of Cursor's core features and workflows
- Tips and tricks for maximizing productivity with AI-assisted coding
- Introduction to Memory Bank for persistent context across sessions
- High energy from attendees with strong interest in AI tools

## Key Takeaways

- The Türkiye developer community has strong appetite for AI-assisted development tools
- Hands-on demos resonate better than theoretical presentations
- Community building starts with showing real value and practical workflows
- Having official Cursor team involvement adds credibility and depth
